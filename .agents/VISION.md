# Omega TV — Vision & Purpose

## The Why

Omega TV is a **Christian television network** founded **33 years ago (1993)** in Iceland. In 2026, it is evolving into a next-generation digital media platform — not just a website, but a vehicle for **transformation of lives and a nation**, honoring the Lord Jesus Christ.

## Mission

To bring the Gospel to Iceland and beyond through the most excellent media possible — reaching **both old and young** — proving what is achievable with media in 2026.

## What This Platform Does

- **Video on Demand** — sermon library, teaching series, documentary content
- **Live Streaming** — daily broadcast, special events
- **Articles & Editorial** — written devotionals, news, long-form content
- **Courses** — discipleship and faith-building education
- **Social Media Publishing** — quote graphics, clips, shareable content
- **Newsletters** — regular communication with the community
- **Prayer** — prayer request community (bænatorg)
- **Testimonials** — stories of God's work in people's lives

## Design Principles

### 1. Trust & Stability
This is a 33-year-old institution. The design must communicate **reliability, credibility, and permanence** — not trendy startup energy. People should feel they are in the hands of something established and trustworthy.

### 2. Beauty
The platform should be **genuinely beautiful** — not "good for a church website," but beautiful by any standard. The excellence of the content deserves excellence in presentation. Beauty itself is a witness.

### 3. Reach Old and Young
The design must be **accessible to older generations** (clear, readable, intuitive) while also being **compelling to younger audiences** (modern, dynamic, engaging). No generation should feel excluded.

### 4. Represent What's Possible in 2026
This should be a **showcase of modern media technology** — not trailing behind secular platforms but matching or exceeding them. Cinematic hero sections, smooth animations, premium typography, intelligent content delivery.

### 5. Transformation, Not Just Information
Every design decision should serve the ultimate goal: **people encountering the living God**. The platform is a tool for transformation — of individuals and of Iceland as a nation.

## The Standard

> The quality of our media should reflect the greatness of the God we serve.

This is not a minimum viable product. This is a **cathedral in digital form** — built with care, craftsmanship, and purpose, to stand for decades and to honor the Lord Jesus Christ.
