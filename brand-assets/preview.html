Mark-only — tiny spaces

The Omega identity system — lockup files saved:

  • brand-assets/omega-mark.svg — mark only (the Ω-in-O)
  • brand-assets/omega-lockup-horizontal.svg — primary lockup: [Ω] MEGA + tagline
  • brand-assets/omega-lockup-stacked.svg — stacked lockup: mark over OMEGA + tagline

The system:

  • Horizontal lockup — website header, Facebook cover, email headers, most everyday contexts
  • Stacked lockup — social profile photos, print footer, contexts with vertical space
  • Mark only — favicons, tiny avatars, broadcast corner ident (16-64px range)

Typography: Source Serif 4 Bold for the wordmark (matches the Ω inside the mark, so MEGA's cap-height aligns with the mark's internal Ω letter). Inter SemiBold caps at 0.22em tracking for the tagline — institutional dignity without competing with the serif wordmark.

The rule that makes it all work: one color, ever. Kerti gold on Night Black (primary). Night Black on Vellum Cream (inverse). Never chrome, never gradient, never glossy. Flat only.
