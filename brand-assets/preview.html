To upload to Facebook:

  1. Open facebook-cover.svg in any browser (or Inkscape)
  2. Export as PNG at 1640 × 720 (2x resolution for retina crispness)
  3. Upload as new cover photo on the Omega Facebook page
  4. Make sure the profile photo is the mark-only version (you can use omega-mark.svg exported at 512×512 or similar)
